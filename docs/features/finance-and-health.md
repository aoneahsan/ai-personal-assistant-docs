# Finance & Health Tools

**The Finance & Health category is AIPA's set of money- and well-being-tracking tools — an expense tracker with a budget planner, a savings tracker, and a health hub covering meditation, mood, sleep, water intake, and exercise.** They give you a simple, private place to log the numbers that shape your day.

## The tools

| Tool | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Finance** | Track expenses and income, and plan a budget. |
| **Savings Tracker** | Track progress toward your savings goals. |
| **Health & Wellness** | A hub for meditation/breathing, mood, sleep, water intake, and exercise logging. |

The **Finance** tool combines expense tracking and budget planning in one place, and the **Health & Wellness** hub groups several well-being trackers (mood, meditation, sleep, water, exercise) so you don't open a separate app for each.

## Common use cases

- **See where money goes.** Log spending and income in **Finance**, set category budgets, and watch how you're tracking against them.
- **Hit a savings target.** Create a goal in the **Savings Tracker** and update progress as you set money aside.
- **Build healthier days.** Log your **mood**, run a **breathing/meditation** session, track **sleep** and **water**, and record **workouts** — all in the Health & Wellness hub.
- **Spot patterns.** Reviewing mood alongside sleep and exercise over time can surface simple, useful correlations.

## Honest notes

- **AIPA is not financial advice.** The finance tools help you record and plan your own money; they don't provide investment, tax, or financial advice.
- **AIPA is not medical advice.** The health trackers are for personal logging and awareness. They are not a medical device and do not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. For health concerns, consult a qualified healthcare professional.
- **You enter the data.** These tools track what you log; they don't connect to your bank or to medical sensors. That keeps them private and free, but it means accuracy depends on what you record.

## FAQ

### Does AIPA connect to my bank?
No. You enter transactions yourself. That keeps your financial data private to your own account and avoids paid banking integrations.

### Is the health hub a medical tool?
No. It's for personal logging and awareness only. It does not diagnose or treat anything; consult a healthcare professional for medical concerns.

### Can I track multiple savings goals?
Yes — create a goal in the Savings Tracker and update its progress over time.

### Does my finance and health data sync?
Yes, when signed in. See [Account & Sync](/getting-started/account-and-sync). It's stored under your own account.
