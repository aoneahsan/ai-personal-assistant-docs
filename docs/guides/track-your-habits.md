# Track your habits

**This guide shows how to build and keep a habit in AIPA using the Habit Tracker — add a habit, check it off each day, and grow a streak.** Habits are part of the [Productivity](/features/productivity) category and sync across your devices when you're signed in.

## Steps

1. **Open the Habit Tracker.** From the Features launcher, search "habits" and open **Habit Tracker**.
2. **Add a habit.** Create a habit with a clear, small, daily action — "Read 10 minutes" beats "Read more."
3. **Check it off.** Each day you do it, mark it complete. Your streak counter increases.
4. **Pair it with a reminder.** Set a [Reminder](/features/productivity) at a consistent time so the habit has a trigger.
5. **Review your streaks.** Watch the streak grow; a visible streak is motivating and shows momentum at a glance.

## Tips

- **Start tiny.** A habit you can do in two minutes is easier to keep than an ambitious one.
- **Stack habits.** Attach a new habit to an existing routine (e.g., "after coffee, journal one line").
- **Don't break the chain twice.** Missing one day happens; the goal is to never miss two in a row.
- **Combine with the [Daily Planner](/guides/plan-your-day).** Put the habit on your day's plan so it's intentional.

## Honest note

The Habit Tracker tracks what you mark. It doesn't auto-detect that you did something — you check it off. That keeps it private and simple, but it relies on your honest input.

## FAQ

### Will I get reminded automatically?
Only if you set a reminder and allow notifications. The tracker itself records completions you mark.

### Do streaks sync across devices?
Yes, when signed in with the same Google account. See [Account & Sync](/getting-started/account-and-sync).

### Can I track more than one habit?
Yes — add as many habits as you want and check each off independently.
