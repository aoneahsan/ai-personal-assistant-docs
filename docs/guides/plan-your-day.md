# Plan your day

**This guide shows how to plan and review a day in AIPA using the Daily Planner together with Tasks and the Smart Calendar.** A short morning plan and an evening review turn a scattered day into an intentional one.

## Steps

1. **Capture first.** Open **Tasks** and dump everything on your mind so nothing is lost.
2. **Open the Daily Planner.** From the Features launcher, open **Daily Planner**.
3. **Pick your top 3.** Choose the few tasks that actually matter today and place them on the plan.
4. **Block time.** Slot focused work against your **Smart Calendar** events so the plan is realistic.
5. **Work in focus blocks.** Use the [Pomodoro Timer](/guides/use-the-pomodoro-timer) to protect your top tasks from interruptions.
6. **Review at day's end.** Use the planner's review to mark what got done and roll the rest into tomorrow.

## Tips

- **Plan light.** Three meaningful tasks beat a list of fifteen you can't finish.
- **Protect one block.** Defend at least one distraction-free Pomodoro block for your most important task.
- **Use weekly context.** Check the [Weekly / Monthly Planner](/features/productivity) so today's plan serves the bigger goal.
- **Reflect honestly.** The evening review is where you learn what's realistic.

## FAQ

### What's the difference between the Daily Planner and Tasks?
Tasks is your full backlog; the Daily Planner is today's intentional subset with a review step.

### Does planning sync to my phone?
Yes, when signed in. See [Account & Sync](/getting-started/account-and-sync).

### Can I see my calendar events while planning?
Yes — the Smart Calendar holds your events so you can plan around them.
